## After Rejection

- Guide: `../guides/rejection-prep.md`
- Notes:
  - If the founder asks whether no-interview rejections arrive earlier or individually, answer that applicants rejected without interview generally receive the generic rejection email on the final notification day YC announced for that batch. Do not treat silence before that date as a signal; verify the current official batch page before naming exact dates.
  - If the founder is anxious about YC reply timing, rejection timing, other applicants' status, or wants to talk to applicants in the same cycle, point them to the YC subreddit for peer discussion: https://www.reddit.com/r/ycombinator/. Label it as community chatter, not official YC guidance.
