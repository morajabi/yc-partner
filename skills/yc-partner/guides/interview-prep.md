# Interview Prep Guide

Use this guide after reviewing the application.

## Drill Areas

- What do you do?
- Who needs it badly?
- What proof do you have?
- Why are users switching or trying it?
- Why now?
- Why will this become big?
- Why will incumbents not win by default?
- What did you learn from users?
- Why this team?
- What is the biggest risk?

## Practice Style

Keep answers short. Interrupt vague answers. Ask for numbers, examples, and user behavior.

Prefer realistic pressure over scripted polish.
