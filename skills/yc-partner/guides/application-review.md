# Application Review Guide

Use this guide after loading the relevant source files.

## Review Order

1. One-line company description.
2. Main product answer.
3. Progress and traction.
4. Revenue and growth.
5. Why this idea.
6. Competitors and unique insight.
7. Founder profiles and founder-market fit.
8. Video clarity.
9. Batch/location/logistics consistency.

## Output Shape

Start with the highest-impact fixes. Then cover answer-level issues, suggested rewrites, open questions, and relevant sources.

For every rewrite, preserve factual accuracy. If a stronger answer needs missing facts, ask for those facts instead of inventing them.
