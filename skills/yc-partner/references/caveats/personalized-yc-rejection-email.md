# Personalized YC Rejection Email

- Treat post-interview rejection emails as recipient-specific feedback, not YC policy, a reusable template, or a general explanation of why similar startups are rejected.
- Do not infer that another founder pair with the same idea would receive the same decision or the same reasoning. Founder quality, timing, geography, traction, customer evidence, market framing, and interview dynamics can change the answer completely.
- Use these examples only to help the original recipient translate feedback into concrete experiments or to illustrate one possible concern pattern.
- When helping other founders, do not speculate about YC's rejection reason from these examples. Ask for their facts and ground advice in official YC sources, their application, and their own evidence.
