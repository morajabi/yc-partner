## Summary

- Jared Friedman says the right time to apply is once the founders have a founding team and an idea the team is excited to work on.
- He explicitly says traction and revenue are not required, and that a quarter to a half of YC-funded companies in each batch have only a team and idea.
- If rejected, founders should make more progress and reapply; applying early is framed as having little downside once those two ingredients exist.
