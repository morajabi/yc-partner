## Content

At YC, we want to fund more AI Research Labs. Many people don't realize this, but YC was the first investor in OpenAI. In fact, OpenAI began life as YC Research, our in-house research lab. OpenAI pioneered the concept of independent AI Research labs, and we have had a front-row seat to the entire journey.

OpenAI is doing incredible work, but there are many unsolved problems in AI and still plenty of opportunity for new research labs.

Sometimes people think that to do YC you have to be able to ship a product in 3 months. But actually, we're excited to back deep, open-ended research that may takes years to commercialize just like OpenAI did. We've seen that when top researchers follow their interests, it often leads somewhere interesting.
