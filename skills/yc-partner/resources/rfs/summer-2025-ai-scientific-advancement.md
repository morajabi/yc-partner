## Content

A lot of the software tools used for scientific applications in chemistry, biology, materials science, or operations research haven't changed much in decades. These rely on standard methods along PhDs to solve complex problems in drug discovery, chemical process optimization, metals & mining, or power grid optimization.

In particular, test-time compute is unlocking new types of startups that can solve these scientific problems.

We'd love to see more startups get created that use AI to transform how physical things are made faster and more efficiently.
