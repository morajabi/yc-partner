Michael Seibel (@mwseibel), Oct 31, 2015:

My top ten pieces of advice for preparing for a YC Interview, good luck! (Michael Seibel, official partner)

1) Interview is 10 minutes long and qoes tast - practice and time yourselt
2) The focus will be on Team, Market, and Traction
3) Most answers should be no longer than 20-30 seconds - tollow up questions will come
4) Don't try to demo first thing - demos are usually in the second half of
the interview
5) Founders shouldn't talk over each other or contradict each other
6) The first question is "what do you do" - have a great 30 second answer that covers what you do with an example.
This is the hardest and most
impactful question - AVOID JARGON - vour mom and dad should be able to understand what you do from this answer.
7) There are websites with sample interview questions - google them and
practice
8) Ouestions will be rapid fire -
vou won't be able to control the
direction of the interview - just roll with it :)
9) when you intro yourselves in the beqinning be clear about which founders
does business and which does tech
10) If there are any red flag/weak points for your startup be ready to answer a question about them with clear 15 second answers.
Be prepared
but don't dwell on the bad.
