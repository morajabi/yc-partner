## Content

Paul Graham (@paulg), Aug 5, 2023:

The biggest myth about YC is that you need to wait till your startup is far enough along to get accepted. Actually it's easier to get in early. Most of YC's big successes weren't launched yet when they applied, so YC partners have a bias toward that kind of company.

Relevant visible follow-up from Paul Graham (@paulg), Aug 5, 2023:

Applying early in a company's life shows confidence, and confidence is a good predictor of how startups will do.
