## Summary

- Paul Graham shares W23 acceptance stats to answer founders who wonder whether they are too early for YC: 77% of the batch had no revenue when accepted, and 52% had only an idea. In replies, he says applying to YC is not random like a lottery ticket, YC cares more about founder progress than company progress, acceptance is not capped, having no idea at all is a bad sign, YC has accepted founders in their 70s, and Africa-based companies do not need more progress than companies from other continents.
