### Reply 1

Paul Graham (@paulg), Jun 5, 2026:

One of the most important claims to be able to make is that they're the current leader in their market. It's surprising how many founders aren't consciously aware of this till I interview it out of them.

### Reply 2

Paul Graham (@paulg), Jun 5, 2026:

This should go without saying, but it's important that startups' claims be not just unequivocal but true as well. That's the point of unequivocal claims: they're definitely either true or false. So (unless you're a pathological liar) they're a way of holding yourself to account.
