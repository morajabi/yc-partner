### Reply 1

Paul Graham (@paulg), Apr 22, 2024:

As YC has become more established, the idea has spread that your startup has to be established in order to be accepted. But that's not true. Initial traction proves something, but not much. The YC partners care more about what you're like than how far along you are.

### Reply 2

Paul Graham (@paulg), Apr 22, 2024:

At YC it's a long-standing tradition, and a point of pride among the partners, to be able to pick startups without having a revenue graph to look at. So if you think you're good, trust them to recognize it. If they fail it won't be for lack of trying.
