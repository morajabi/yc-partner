## Summary

- Paul Graham says founders have been advised wrongly if they were told not to apply to YC unless they are generating revenue.
- He says none of the most successful startups YC funded had significant revenue when they applied, and most had zero revenue.
- He notes he qualified "significant" because Airbnb had a trickle of revenue, though without growth.
