### Application

#### Founders

- Who writes code, or does other technical work on your product? Was any of it done by a non-founder? Please explain.
- Are you looking for a cofounder?

#### Founder Video

- Please record a one minute video introducing the founder(s).*
  - Helper text: Read more about the video here. Make sure the file does not exceed 100 MB.

#### Company

- Company name*
- Describe what your company does in 50 characters or less.*
- Company URL, if any
- If you have a demo, attach it below.
  - Helper text: Anything that shows us how the product works. Please limit to 3 minutes / 100 MB.
- Please provide a link to the product, if any.
- If login credentials are required for the link above, enter them here.
- What is your company going to make? Please describe your product and what it does or will do.
- Where do you live now, and where would the company be based after YC?
  - Helper text: Use the format City A, Country A / City B, Country B.
- Explain your decision regarding location.

#### Progress

- How far along are you?
- How long have each of you been working on this? How much of that has been full-time? Please explain.
- What tech stack are you using, or planning to use, to build this product? Include AI models and AI coding tools you use.
- Optional: attach a coding agent session you're particularly proud of.
  - Helper text: This is an experimental question for the Fall 2026 batch to give people a chance to show off their skills with AI coding tools.
  - Helper text: Many coding agents (e.g. Claude Code, Cursor, etc.) have a `/export` command, or otherwise include a button allowing you to export a transcript. Can be text or markdown.
  - Upload constraint: Click or drag to upload a `.md` or `.txt` file. Maximum file size: 25MB.
- Are people using your product?
  - Options: Yes / No
- Do you have revenue?
  - Options: Yes / No
- If you are applying with the same idea as a previous batch, did anything change? If you applied with a different idea, why did you pivot and what did you learn from the last idea?
- If you have already participated or committed to participate in an incubator, "accelerator" or "pre-accelerator" program, please tell us about it.

#### Idea

- Why did you pick this idea to work on? Do you have domain expertise in this area? How do you know people need what you're making?
- Who are your competitors? What do you understand about your business that they don't?
- How do or will you make money? How much could you make?
  - Helper text: We realize you can't know precisely, but give your best estimate.
- If you had any other ideas you considered applying with, please list them. One may be something we've been waiting for. Often when we fund people it's to do something they list here and not in the main application.

#### Equity

- Have you formed ANY legal entity yet?
  - Helper text: This may be in the United States, in your home country or in another country.
  - Options: Yes / No
- Have you taken any investment yet?
  - Options: Yes / No
- Are you currently fundraising?
  - Options: Yes / No

#### Curious

- What convinced you to apply to Y Combinator? Did someone encourage you to apply? Have you been to any YC events?
- How did you hear about Y Combinator?

#### Batch Preference

- What batch do you want to apply for?
  - Options: Fall 2026 / A batch after Fall 2026

### Founder Profile

#### Basics

- Name*
- Email*
- Date of birth*
- Phone Number*
  - Helper text: We may use this number to call or text you about your application. International numbers will be called with WhatsApp.
- Gender
- City where you currently live*

#### Role & Responsibilities

- What is your title, or if you haven’t set it yet, main responsibility?*
  - Helper text: CEO, CTO, Sales, Engineering, etc.
- What percent equity do you have?*
  - Helper text: If you haven't incorporated yet, give the equity percent you expect to have.
- Are you a technical founder?*
  - Helper text: You are a programmer, engineer, or scientist who can build the product without outside assistance.
  - Options: Yes / No
- Are you currently in school?
  - Options: Yes / No
- If accepted to YC, will you commit to working exclusively on this project for the next year?*
  - Helper text: i.e., you won't be in school or have another job.
  - Options: Yes / No

#### Background

- Your LinkedIn URL*
- Education*
  - Helper text: Include your high school, college and any advanced degrees. Should you be accepted, we may ask for academic transcripts.
- Work History*
  - Helper text: Should you be accepted, we may ask for references.

#### Social Media

- Personal website
- Github URL
- Twitter URL

#### Accomplishments

- Please tell us about a time you most successfully hacked some (non-computer) system to your advantage.
- Please tell us in one or two sentences about the most impressive thing other than this startup that you have built or achieved.
- Tell us about things you've built before. For example apps you’ve built, websites, open source contributions. Include URLs if possible.
- List any competitions/awards you have won, or papers you’ve published.

#### Conditional Questions For Founders 25 Or Under

- List any relevant or impressive test scores.
- List any entrepreneurship programs, clubs, or hacker houses you have participated in or are currently participating in.
