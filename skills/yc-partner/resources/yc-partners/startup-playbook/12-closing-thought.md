## Essay Text

Remember that at least a thousand people have every great idea. One of them actually becomes
successful. The difference comes down to execution. It's a grind, and everyone wishes there were
some other way to transform "idea" into "success", but no one has figured it out yet.

So all you need is a great idea, a great team, a great product, and great execution. So easy!;)
