## Transcript

You don't need to know anyone at YC to get in. There's some bad advice on the internet that says you
need a YC alumni recommendation. That is not the case. If a YC alumni knows you well and wants to
chime in on your behalf, we'll gladly read it. But hundreds of startups getting every batch without
a recommendation. And it's actually a negative signal when founders solicit a bunch of
recommendations from alumni who barely know them. It can come across as spammy, so I just wouldn't
do it.
