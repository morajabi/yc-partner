## Transcript

You should apply to YC when you're working on a startup and you think that your company would
benefit from YC. There are many ways that YC can accelerate a startup's growth, and it's up to you
as a founder to decide if and when YC is right for you. There's no such thing as too early or too
late. Our application is always open and we read applications year round, so apply whenever the time
is right for your company.
