## Summary

- Stephanie Simon says more founders should include a product demo in the YC application.
- The demo can be unpolished, local, or as rough as a CSV; the important difference is showing that something exists rather than only an idea.
- Use when reviewing application hygiene, product evidence, demo links, and founder seriousness.

## Transcript

One thing that I wish more founders would include on their application is a demo of their product.
So many applications we get or ideas, someone's been thinking about for a year with nothing done. We
don't care if the demo is unpolished. We don't care if you're still running it locally or if it's
just a CSV right now. The difference between nothing and something is actually huge to us. It shows
us that you're serious and that you can get something built.
