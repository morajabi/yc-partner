## Page Text

## President & CEO

- Garry Tan — President & CEO. Former YC partner, co-founder of Initialized Capital and Posterous (YC S08), formerly at Palantir.

## Managing Partners

- Jared Friedman — Managing Partner. Cofounder of Scribd (YC S06); studied computer science at Harvard.
- Diana Hu — Managing Partner. Cofounder and CTO of Escher Reality (YC S17), acquired by Niantic; later led Niantic's AR platform.
- Harj Taggar — Managing Partner. Founder/CEO of Triplebyte (YC S15) and Auctomatic (YC W07); YC partner since 2010 with a gap to build Triplebyte.

## General Partners

- Gustaf Alströmer — General Partner. Former Airbnb growth product lead; cofounder/CEO of Heysan (YC W07).
- Harshita Arora — General Partner. Cofounder of AtoB (YC S20), a trucking financial infrastructure company; previously built and sold Crypto Price Tracker.
- Tom Blomfield — General Partner. Cofounder of Monzo and GoCardless (YC S11).
- Tyler Bosmeny — General Partner. Cofounder and CEO of Clever (YC S12), acquired in 2021.
- Nicolas Dessaigne — General Partner. Cofounder and former CEO of Algolia (YC W14).
- Aaron Epstein — General Partner. Cofounder of Creative Market (YC W10).
- Brad Flora — General Partner. Cofounder and CEO of Perfect Audience, funded by YC in 2011 and acquired by Marin Software.
- Ankit Gupta — General Partner. Cofounder of Reverie Labs, acquired by Ginkgo Bioworks in 2024; machine learning and drug discovery background.
- Pete Koomen — General Partner. Cofounder of Optimizely (YC W10).
- David Lieb — General Partner. Cofounder and CEO of Bump (YC S09), acquired by Google; Bump's unreleased photo app became the foundation of Google Photos.
- Andrew Miklas — General Partner. Cofounder and founding CTO of PagerDuty (YC S10, NYSE: PD); later early-stage investor at s28 Capital.
- Jon Xu — General Partner. Cofounder and former CTO of FutureAdvisor (YC S10), acquired by BlackRock.

## Founders, Retired

- Trevor Blackwell — Founder, Retired. Roboticist and computer scientist; built an early dynamically balancing biped robot.
- Paul Graham — Founder, Retired. Cofounder of Viaweb with Robert Morris; author of *On Lisp*, *ANSI Common Lisp*, and *Hackers & Painters*.
- Jessica Livingston — Founder, Retired. Author of *Founders at Work*; formerly VP of marketing at Adams Harkness.
- Robert Morris — Founder, Retired. MIT computer science professor; cofounded Viaweb with Paul Graham.
