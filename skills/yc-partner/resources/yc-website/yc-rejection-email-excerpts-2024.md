### Screenshot A: General Rejection + Solo Founder Variant

Subject: Your Y Combinator Application

From: Y Combinator `<apply@ycombinator.com>`

Relevant text:

> Thanks for applying to Y Combinator. We're sorry to say that your startup was not selected to interview for the upcoming Y Combinator batch. We carefully reviewed thousands of applications and since there's a limit on the number of startups we can interview, we had to turn away a lot of promising companies.

> Unfortunately we can't give you individual feedback about your application. This page explains why.

> We hope you reapply for the next batch; in fact, we encourage it. The next batch will run from January-March 2025 and the deadline to apply for it is in October 2024. Applying multiple times does not count against you and a surprisingly large number of companies are funded after applying more than once. Over 50% of the startups we accept are repeat applicants.

> It looks like you applied as a solo founder. Having a co-founder greatly improves your chances of startup success, and, therefore, also your chances of getting into YC. If you're open to finding a co-founder, check out our co-founder-matching platform! YC has actually funded dozens of teams that met through co-founder matching. You can sign up at ycombinator.com/cofounder-matching.

> Lastly, we find that many strong YC founders have learned how to start a startup by working at one. YC runs workatastartup.com to help you connect with founders and find a job at over 700 fast-growing and well-funded YC startups.
